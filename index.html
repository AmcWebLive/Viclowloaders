About Us

Built for
the Big Stuff

Vic Low Loaders is a trusted heavy haulage and transportation service based in Pakenham, Victoria. We specialise in moving the loads that others can't — oversized plant, heavy machinery, and construction equipment across Australia.

From our base at 21 Industrial Drive, Pakenham, we run jobs in every state and territory. Our experienced team and purpose-built equipment mean your load gets there on time, every time.
